Assignment 1 - Airtable

There is a Chinese quote “Every boy has a toy car in his heart.” I am definitely one of the boys that is extremely obsessed with cars. I read car magazines every month in order to update the news for any new cars, and I can recognize almost every cars just with a glance of a part of them.

How passionate with cars am I? The story starts when I was a little boy. When I was very young I realized that I am really obsessed with cars, and I started observing every car passing by whenever and wherever I am. I tried my best to recognize the brands of cars, and after a long period of self-training, I could even know the features of each brands and recognize which brand or which model that just passed by with a glance. I was dying to experience the feeling of driving a car and stepping on the pedal, and I begged and cried to my dad just for a try.

In my 5th grade, I drove a real car the first time at a parking lot with no cars in it with my nervous dad in the passenger seat. I can still clearly remember the scene when I drove the Volkswagen Golf in the parking lot turning corners and practicing to control the brake to stop smoothly. Until now, I still enjoy every ride on the road when I am back in Taiwan.

The passion of cars is the reason why I decided to research about the car brands in Taiwan. The data below are all researched by myself! By the way, I gained some knowledge about some rare brands as I was doing research for this task. It is so interesting!
